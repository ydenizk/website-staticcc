DIGITAL BRAND BUILDING

We combine our strong expertise in (digital) brand building with a value-based marketing approach and scale this both to our own brand portfolio, consisting of eight innovative premium brands, and to brands of partners who trust our expertise.

MULTI-CHANNEL-DISTRIBUTION

We sell our brands and products directly to more than 1,000,000 end customers through our four online shops and to more than 13,000 stationary point-of-sales through our B2B Shop and our strong sales team.

BRAND PLATFORM SERVICES & SCALING

As a technology-driven brand platform, we provide our brands with a broad portfolio of shared services, for example in the areas of technology, business intelligence, marketing, brand building, end customer sales (D2C) and B2B logistics.
